XAUUSD (Gold/US Dollar) represents the spot price of one troy ounce of gold in US dollars, acting as a premier, highly liquid, and volatile instrument in the Forex market . Often viewed as a safe-haven asset, its price is driven by global economic uncertainty, inflation expectations, and geopolitical tensions.

Trading 24/5, this pair typically exhibits an inverse relationship with the US Dollar, often surging when the greenback weakens or during market turmoil. It is widely used by traders for hedging against risk and capitalizing on market volatility, especially around major economic releases like US Nonfarm Payrolls (NFP) and Federal Reserve interest rate decisions.
